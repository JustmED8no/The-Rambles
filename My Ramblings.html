Acting as a warning to avoid or invitation to watch for all thoses who use the site or simply want a good anime. MAL's power is so strong, that it's ratings of anime are trusted more by the anime watching community than the official sites some of the anime themselves were originally posted on. And of course, this power is not false, as, because of te huge user base and the ever growing population of anime watchers joining the clan, the ratings on anime on MAL are nothing but perfect. Or, at least very accurate. Using a 1-10 system, animes can be 1-very bad,2-bad,3-Meh,4-boring,5-OK,6-Good,7-great,8-awesome,9-perfect and 10, masterpiece.
